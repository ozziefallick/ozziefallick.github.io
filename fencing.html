After having fenced very briefly in elementary school, I picked it up again at the beginning of my freshman year of college (2010) at the University of Maryland Fencing Club. I was a starter on the sabre squad beginning in my sophomore year, and was elected president for the 2012-2013 season in my junior year. I am captain of the sabre squad.

With the team, I compete in the Baltimore-Washington Collegiate Fencing Conference (BWCFC) and the Mid-Atlantic College Fencing Association (MACFA), as well as the Chaos Invitational, which we host, the Temple Open, and the United States Association of Collegiate Fencing Clubs (USACFC) national championships.

I won silver in the 2012 Chaos Invitational and gold in the 2013 and 2014 BWCFC Championships.
